About Craigslist

Craigslist provides local classifieds and forums for jobs, housing, for sale, services, local community, and events.

History

Craig Newmark began the service in 1995 as an email distribution list to friends, featuring local events in the San Francisco Bay Area. It became a web-based service in 1996 and expanded into other classified categories. With the success of the service, Craigslist was incorporated in 1999, and it expanded to other cities beyond San Francisco.

Mission

Craigslist's mission is to help people connect through classifieds and community forums. We aim to build an online platform that is straightforward and effective for users seeking to find or post job listings, housing, services, goods for sale, and local events.

Community

Craigslist is committed to supporting and empowering local communities. We provide a platform where users can post about jobs, housing, items for sale, services, and local events. Our goal is to facilitate genuine connections within communities, helping people find what they need and fostering positive interactions.

Accessibility

Craigslist is designed to be accessible to as many people as possible. The platform is simple and straightforward, with a focus on ease of use. We strive to ensure that our services are available to everyone, regardless of technical skills or internet access.

Trust and Safety

Craigslist prioritizes the safety and trust of our users. We have systems in place to monitor and remove fraudulent or inappropriate postings, and we encourage our users to report any suspicious activity. Our commitment is to provide a safe, reliable platform for all users.

Corporate Information

Craigslist, Inc. is a privately held company based in San Francisco, California. Despite its global reach, Craigslist remains a small, tightly-knit team focused on serving the needs of local communities. Our business model is simple: we charge minimal fees for job postings and certain other categories to cover our operating costs.
